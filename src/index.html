Welcome Back!

August 20, 2018

Hello all and welcome to another school year! I am so excited to have your child in my classroom! I will be trying to post bi-weekly or so on my blog to keep you updated on things happening in the classroom.

Items needed to be successful in class: notebook, paper, writing utensils.

We will be doing Readers Workshop again this year. Students will need to bring a book to read during silent reading time. I have plenty that they can check out from my own library or the Handy library. They also may bring one in from home. I would like them to have these by September 4th.

If you have any questions, as always you can email me through Skyward or my email is englishk@bcschools.net.

~Mrs. English
