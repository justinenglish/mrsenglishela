ABOUT ME

My name is Kari English. I have my Bachelor of Arts Degree in Secondary Education and English Language Arts from Alma College. I graduated in 2008. I have been teaching at Handy Middle School since December of 2016. I came to Handy from Bay Arenac Community High School, where I worked for five years. I have had the pleasure of teaching 7th grade ELA and Intervention at Handy.

I have been married to my husband since September 2016 and we welcomed our first little boy in January 2018. My hobbies include, hanging out with my family, going camping in Northern Michigan (Boyne City is my favorite), watching hockey (especially the Pittsburgh Penguins), playing tennis and reading. Some of my favorite books include To Kill a Mockingbird and The Outsiders.

I hope you learned a little bit more about me. I look forward to the coming year!

Welcome Back!

August 20, 2018

Hello all and welcome to another school year! I am so excited to have your child in my classroom! I will be trying to post bi-weekly or so on my blog to keep you updated on things happening in the classroom.

Items needed to be successful in class: notebook, paper, writing utensils.

We will be doing Readers Workshop again this year. Students will need to bring a book to read during silent reading time. I have plenty that they can check out from my own library or the Handy library. They also may bring one in from home. I would like them to have these by September 4th.

If you have any questions, as always you can email me through Skyward or my email is englishk@bcschools.net.

~Mrs. English
